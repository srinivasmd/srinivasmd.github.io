Directions:

Welcome to the laser shoot portion of the test! Please read all of these directions because they are important to completing the simulation. Also, remember to take a screenshot and submit it on the Google form once you finish. Here are the instructions:

  • You will have four minutes to complete the simulation once you press the start button
  • Once the time runs out or you turn on the laser it will lock all of the mirrors in their current position
  • There are three ways to move mirrors: click and drag, arrow keys for precision movement, or manually enter coordinates
  • To rotate mirrors, hold shift and drag, press shift and arrow keys for precision roation, or manually enter angle
  • The path of the laser is marked thrugh the center, and the target is the arrow
  • Please note that mirrors will not move with the grid when you zoom
  • Good luck!
